CURO — Privacy Policy

App: CURO (com.parul.commute.pu_commute)  ·  Developer: Parul University Transport (ORM)
Last updated: 9 July 2026

CURO ("the app", "we", "us") is a closed-network carpooling app for verified staff of Parul University. This policy explains what data we collect, why, how it is used and shared, and the choices you have.

Contact / Data controller: Parul University Transport (ORM) — orm@paruluniversity.ac.in

Who can use CURO

CURO is restricted to Parul University staff with an @paruluniversity.ac.in account. You must sign in with your university Google account or an admin-issued CURO account (email and password).

Information we collect

You provide:

  • Account & profile: name, university email, phone number, designation, department, gender, profile photo, home pickup zone and commute preferences.
  • Vehicle (drivers): model, colour, registration, vehicle type and seats.
  • Ride & booking activity: rides you offer or request, pickup/drop points, notes you add, chat messages, and ratings.

Collected automatically (only while you use the relevant feature):

  • Location: your device's GPS location is used to (a) publish a driver's live position while they are offering an active ride, (b) show nearby drivers on the map, (c) set a precise pickup point, and (d) let you share your live location with a contact. Location is accessed only while the app is in use — CURO does not collect background location, and a driver's location sharing stops when the app is backgrounded or the trip ends.
  • Device notifications token for delivering in-app alerts.

How we use your information

  • Match passengers with drivers on overlapping routes and times.
  • Show driver/passenger identity and contact details to each other only after a booking is accepted, so you can coordinate the trip.
  • Deliver in-app chat between riders and drivers.
  • Send ride-related notifications (requests, acceptances, cancellations, messages).
  • Estimate fuel-share cost and sustainability stats.
  • Operate safety features (SOS, behaviour reports) and enforce the no-show / cancellation policy.

How your information is shared

  • With other CURO users: your name, photo, designation/department and ride details are visible to relevant users; phone numbers are revealed only to the matched driver and passenger after a booking is accepted.
  • Service providers: we use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore) to store data and operate the app; OpenStreetMap / OSRM / Nominatim for maps, routing and place names. Map/route requests include coordinates necessary to render the route.
  • Transport admin: SOS alerts and behaviour reports are shared with Parul University Transport for safety investigation.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. CURO does not process any in-app payments.

Data retention & account deletion

  • Your data is retained while your account is active.
  • You can delete your CURO account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app: open Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, vehicle, rides, bookings, notifications and chat identity from our systems and deletes your authentication record. There is no additional retention period.
  • You may also request deletion by emailing orm@paruluniversity.ac.in from your university address; requests are honoured within 30 days.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Google Firebase, protected by authentication and access rules. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children

CURO is for university staff and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be notified in the app or by email. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions or requests: orm@paruluniversity.ac.in
